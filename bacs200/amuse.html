Mildly Amusing

by Josh Flatt

The internet is a place that someone can find just about anything on, whether that means
information for businesses, making appointments, or some very dark interests. Alternatively,
the internet is also full of humor. Below are a few of my favorite web pages that I get a good
kick out of.

Dad Jokes

One of my favorite kinds of jokes is Dad Jokes. These are the jokes
that are funny to the person who says them, or funny because of just how bad they are.

One great place to find a bunch of these top-tier jokes and puns is on Twitter.

<Sometimes> Fresh Laughs

Mildly Amusing

by Josh Flatt

The internet is a place that someone can find just about anything on, whether that means
information for businesses, making appointments, or some very dark interests. Alternatively,
the internet is also full of humor. Below are a few of my favorite web pages that I get a good
kick out of.

Dad Jokes

One of my favorite kinds of jokes is Dad Jokes. These are the jokes
that are funny to the person who says them, or funny because of just how bad they are.

One great place to find a bunch of these top-tier jokes and puns is on Twitter.

Dad Jokes

Premature Celebrations

Something that is hilarious to watch is someone celebrating an accomplishment they're about to reach,
only to mess up their secured first place.
This is when someone in a race slows down to celebrate, only to be passed, or a wide receiver celebrating
a touchdown before scoring it, only to have the ball snatched out of their hands.
Below is a link to a subreddit full of these fails.

Premature Celebrations

Mirrors For Sale!!!

Something that I turn to whenever I'm not having the best of days is googling people selling mirrors.
Sometimes, the person selling the mirror can be seen wearing something absolutely hideous, but
the best of these photographs are when the people try to hide or mask themselves in the reflection.

The Million Dollar Highway

This road is a challenge. It climbs over 3 passes over 10,000 feet and is considered to be the most dangerous route in Colorado, due to steep drop-offs and unpredictable weather. If you feel up to an adventure, travel one of the most beautiful roads in the world.

Ghost Towns & Gold Mining

The San Juan mountains have a deep history of old-western gold mining. When the Pike's Peak gold rush began in 1858, many, many people flocked to this area of the Rockies in search of gold. Subsequently, many towns sprung up, only to die with the gold rush craze.
Here's some remnants of history...

  • Animas Forks
  • Red Mountain Town
  • Buffalo Boy Mine
  • Ironton
